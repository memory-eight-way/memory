NURSERY RHYMES
Jack and Jill went up the hill,

1. Jack and Jill went up the hill,

2. To fetch a pail of water;

3. Jack fell down, and broke his crown

4. And Jill came tumbling after.

5. Up Jack got and home did trot

6. As fast as he could caper;

7. Went to bed to mend his head

8. With vinegar and brown paper.

9. Jill came in and she did grin,

10. To see his paper plaster,

11. Mother, vexed, did whip her next,

12. For causing Jack's disaster.
