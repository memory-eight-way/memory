NURSERY RHYMES
This is the way the ladies ride--

1. This is the way the ladies ride--

2. Saddle-a-side, saddle-a-side!

3. This is the way the gentlemen ride--

4. Sitting astride, sitting astride!

5. This is the way the grandmothers ride--

6. Bundled and tied, bundled and tied!

7. This is the way the babykins ride--

8. Snuggled inside, snuggled inside!
