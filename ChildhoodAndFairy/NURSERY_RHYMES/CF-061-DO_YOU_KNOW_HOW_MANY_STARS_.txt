NURSERY RHYMES
DO YOU KNOW HOW MANY STARS?

1. Do you know how many stars There are shining in the skies?

2. Do you know how many clouds Ev'ry day go floating by?

3. God in heaven has counted all, He would miss one should it fall.

4. Do you know how many children Go to little beds at night, And without a care or sorrow, Wake up in the morning light?

5. God in heaven each name can tell, Loves you, too, and loves you well.
