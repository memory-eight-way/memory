NURSERY RHYMES
Simple Simon met a pieman,

1. Simple Simon met a pieman,

2. Going to the fair;

3. Says Simple Simon to the pieman,

4. "Let me taste your ware."

5. Says the pieman to Simple Simon,

6. "Show me first your penny."

7. Says Simple Simon to the pieman,

8. "Indeed I have not any."

9. Simple Simon went a-fishing

10. For to catch a whale;

11. But all the water he could find

12. Was in his mother's pail!
