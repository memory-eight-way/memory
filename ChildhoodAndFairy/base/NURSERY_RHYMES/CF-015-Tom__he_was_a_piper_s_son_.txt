NURSERY RHYMES
Tom, he was a piper's son,

1. Tom, he was a piper's son,

2. He learned to play when he was young,

3. And all the tune that he could play

4. Was, "Over the hills and far away,"

5. Over the hills, and a great way off,

6. The wind will blow my top-knot off.

7. Now, Tom with his pipe made such a noise

8. That he well pleased both the girls and boys,

9. And they always stopped to hear him play

10. "Over the hills and far away."
