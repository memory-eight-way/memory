NURSERY RHYMES
BABY-LAND

1. Which is the way to Baby-Land?

2. Any one can tell; Up one flight, To your right; Please to ring the bell.

3. What can you see in Baby-Land?

4. Little folks in white, Downy heads, Cradle-beds, Faces pure and bright.

5. What do they do in Baby-Land?

6. Dream and wake and play, Laugh and crow, Shout and grow, Jolly times have they.

7. What do they say in Baby-Land?

8. Why, the oddest things; Might as well Try to tell What a birdie sings.

9. Who is the Queen of Baby-Land?

10. Mother kind and sweet; And her love, Born above, Guides the little feet.

11. GEORGE COOPER
