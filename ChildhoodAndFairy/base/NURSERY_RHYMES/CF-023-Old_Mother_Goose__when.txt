NURSERY RHYMES
Old Mother Goose, when

1. Old Mother Goose, when

2. She wanted to wander,

3. Would ride through the air

4. On a very fine gander.

5. Mother Goose had a house,

6. 'T was built in a wood,

7. Where an owl at the door

8. For sentinel stood.

9. She had a son Jack,

10. A plain-looking lad;

11. He was not very good,

12. Nor yet very bad.

13. She sent him to market,

14. A live goose he bought:

15. "Here! mother," says he,

16. "It will not go for nought."

17. Jack's goose and her gander

18. Grew very fond;

19. They'd both eat together,

20. Or swim in one pond.

21. Jack found one morning,

22. As I have been told,

23. His goose had laid him

24. An egg of pure gold.

25. Jack rode to his mother,

26. The news for to tell.

27. She called him a good boy,

28. And said it was well.
