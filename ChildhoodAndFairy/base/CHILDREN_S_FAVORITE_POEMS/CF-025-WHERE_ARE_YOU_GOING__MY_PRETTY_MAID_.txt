CHILDREN'S FAVORITE POEMS
WHERE ARE YOU GOING, MY PRETTY MAID?

1. "Where are you going, my pretty maid?"

2. "I am going a-milking, sir," she said.

3. "May I go with you, my pretty maid?"

4. "You're kindly welcome, sir," she said.

5. "What is your father, my pretty maid?"

6. "My father's a farmer, sir," she said.

7. "What is your fortune, my pretty maid?"

8. "My face is my fortune, sir," she said.

9. "Then I won't marry your my pretty maid."

10. "Nobody asked you, sir," she said.
